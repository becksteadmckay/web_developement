Top 10 Super-Hero Movies Ranked

1. The Dark Knight (2008)

Key Actors:

Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, and Aaron Eckhart

Director:

Christopher Nolan

Movie Length:

2h 32m

The Dark Knight has a depth that other superhero movies lack. There are times in the movie when it doesn't feel like a superhero movie but a real-life situation. It is filled with thought-provoking themes that can get anyone's mind turning. One theme this movie has is chaos. The Joker causes chaos in Gotham. Heath Ledger's performance of the Joker was remarkable for anyone to watch. The Joker may be one of the most compelling villains in a superhero movie. Someone who just wants to watch the world burn.

2. Spider-Man 2 (2004)

Key Actors:

Tobey Maguire, Kirsten Dunst, James Franco and Alfred Molina

Director:

Sam Raimi

Movie Length:

2h 07m

Spider-Man 2 is great. It has my favorite scene out of any superhero movie ever. Spider-Man chases the villain Doc-Oc while trying to stop a train. Spider-man heroically sacrifices all his energy and strength to stop the train then passes out. The passengers of the train catch him before he falls. The scene becomes iconic when the passengers lift Spider-Man above them passing him to the next passenger and so forth down the train. I love this movie because of the complex relationship between Mary Jane and Peter Parker. Peter Parker doesn't want to be spider-man so he can spend time with Mary Jane. It highlights a real personal conflict in Peter Parker's life. Peter Parker eventually sacrifices his personal life to become Spider-Man.

3. Thor Ragnarok (2017)

Key Actors:

Chris Hemsworth, Tom Hiddleston, Mark Ruffalo and Cate Blanchett

Director:

Taika Waititi

Movie Length:

2h 10m

Thor Ragnarok made my top three because it's entertaining and funny. Director Taika Waititi decided to take the character of Thor in a new direction. Thor had previously had long gold locks in a movie with a serious attitude. They also took away Thor's Hammer making him the God of Thunder, not the God of Hammers. I loved the new Thor with a little bit of humor. I think everyone likes this Thor more because this movie outperformed the other Thor movies at the box office. The reinvented Thor was a blast to watch. Another thing this movie does well is it has an unexpected plot. I hate movies that are predictable and follow the same superhero movie archetype. This movie was a present surprise. I didn't expect the movie's ending.
